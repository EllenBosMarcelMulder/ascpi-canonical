## Abstract

This document discloses a structural architecture in which **all phase transitions are necessarily crossed-linked**. A phase transition is defined not as a local, time-indexed event, nor as an internally caused state change, but exclusively as a **relational intersection** between inward and outward system dynamics, mediated through a present interaction and constrained by a fixed external reference.

What is commonly interpreted as noise, randomness, or spontaneous emergence in other systems is shown here to be **unmodeled external potency** becoming locally resonant at a crossed-link intersection. Phase transitions may therefore appear sudden or discontinuous, yet remain **structurally computable** without time prediction, probability, or decision logic.

This disclosure establishes crossed-linking as a **necessary and sufficient condition** for phase transition across physical, computational, informational, biological, and cognitive domains, thereby removing novelty from claims that treat phase transitions as locally controllable, decidable, or ownable computational objects.

## 3. Definition of Phase Transition (Corrected)

A phase transition is **not**:

- a step in time,

- a discrete state change,

- a probabilistic jump,

- a locally caused event,

- a decision or trigger.

A phase transition **is**:

> *A relational reconfiguration occurring at the intersection of inward and outward system dynamics, constrained by a reference asymmetry and instantiated through present interaction.*

There is no “before” and “after” in a fundamental sense—only **reconfiguration of relation**.

## 4. The Crossed-Link Invariant

### 4.1 What Crossed-Linked Means

A crossed-link is the **simultaneous validity** of:

- inward dynamics (contraction, coherence, binding),

- outward dynamics (expansion, variation, potential),

- a present relational intersection (the “now”),

- an external reference asymmetry (orientation without metric).

This intersection is **not optional**.  
It is not a layer, a mode, or a switch.

> **A phase exists only insofar as this intersection exists.**

---

### 4.2 What Crossed-Link Is Not

The crossed-link is not:

- memory,

- feedback control,

- recursion,

- causation,

- stochastic fluctuation,

- observer choice.

It is a **structural condition**, not a mechanism.
